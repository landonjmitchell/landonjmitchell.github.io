Mind Reader

I love reading about brains. They are among my favorite mysteries, and perhaps nearer to solutions than many others.

I like learning about the different clues gleaned from neurological research and case studies, as well as the complex networks of electrical and chemical signals, with their many frequencies and patterns. I especially enjoy learning about theories and models of intelligence, biological and otherwise.

Disaster Junkie

I spent a little over two years total working in disaster response, first in Haiti after the earthquake, later in Long Island following Hurricane Sandy.

They were challenging, complicated, engaging experiences. Long-termers and serial returners were sometimes called disaster junkies. Many of my colleagues from that time still work in the field to this day. I still hope to work with some of them again in the future.
